Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Overview

ZZTV Studio is a browser-based video creation service that helps users generate, edit, render, and optionally send videos to connected social platforms. This policy explains what information is processed when you use ZZTV Studio.

Information we process

  • Content you enter or upload, including scripts, images, video clips, audio, titles, captions, and hashtags.
  • Basic TikTok account information you authorize, such as display name, account identifier, and profile image.
  • TikTok authorization tokens needed to keep your account connected and upload videos you choose to send.
  • Technical information normally provided by a browser or hosting service, such as IP address, device type, browser type, request time, and error logs.

How information is used

Information is used only to provide and secure the service, create requested media, maintain your authorized TikTok connection, upload videos at your direction, diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

TikTok data

ZZTV requests only the TikTok permissions required for basic account identification and user-initiated video upload. TikTok access tokens are stored in an encrypted, secure, HTTP-only cookie. ZZTV does not sell TikTok data, use it for advertising profiles, or post without a user action.

Local and third-party processing

Much of the video-rendering workflow runs directly in your browser. Some features may use third-party services selected by the user or configured by the service, including Vercel for hosting, ElevenLabs for narration, Wikimedia Commons for licensed media, and TikTok for account connection and video upload. Those providers process information under their own privacy terms.

Retention and deletion

Locally generated project data may remain in your browser until you clear it. Authentication information remains until it expires, you disconnect TikTok, or it is otherwise deleted. You may revoke ZZTV access at any time from your TikTok account settings. Service logs may be retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting.

Sharing

ZZTV does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate requested features, when required by law, or to protect users, the service, or the public from fraud, abuse, or security threats.

Your choices

You can avoid connecting TikTok, disconnect it through ZZTV, revoke access through TikTok, remove locally stored projects by clearing your browser data, and choose not to upload personal media.

Children

ZZTV Studio is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the service changes. The effective date above will be revised when material updates are made.
